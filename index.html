Integrations and APIs


Marty has two main different modes of operation - remotely controlled with just a Rick in the head (how Marty is normally when you first get it), and an extended mode where a Raspberry Pi, Arduino or other serial-enabled microcontroller or mini-pc is inside the head with Rick.

Some of our APIs and Integrations work with only one of these, some with both so we've split them into those groups below. Martys can also be simulated using V-REP.

Hardware


This set of articles cover the more nitty-gritty technical details of Rick, Marty's control board and the associated electronics they come with. Also covered are complete connection details for interfacing with Rick's GPIOs and interface connectors.


Connection Guide

Pinouts from Rick and information on how to connect a RaspberryPi, Arduino or other serial-enabled device to Rick inside a Marty's head.

Specifications

Rick's maximum power output ratings, battery safety, thermal characteristics, limits and charge capablity.

Schematics

Full schematics for the Rick control board and its common connections to peripherals such as serial ports, GPIOs, Motors and cabling.
